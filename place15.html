A Glimpse into History
Chandragiri, founded in the 11th century, flourished under the Yadava rulers and became the Vijayanagara Empire's capital, showcasing a rich history of valor, diplomacy, and cultural evolution.

Architectural Splendour
Chandragiri Fort, blending Dravidian and Indo-Islamic styles, features massive walls and intricate carvings. The Raja Mahal and Rani Mahal within showcase regal elegance, with stucco work, frescoes, and delicate arches.

Cultural and Spiritual Significance
Chandragiri is a spiritual haven with ancient temples, notably the Chandragiri Temple dedicated to Lord Venkateswara. The Brahmotsavam festival features vibrant processions, rituals, and a grand chariot procession.
