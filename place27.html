A Glimpse into History
The Parthasarathy Temple, dating to the 8th century Pallava era, is tied to the Mahabharata and Bhakti movement, with patronage from Cholas and Vijayanagar rulers highlighting its religious significance.

Architectural Splendour
The temple epitomizes Dravidian architecture with its majestic gopuram, intricate carvings, vimanam, and serene prakarams, creating a sacred space for religious and cultural activities with exquisite stone work.
