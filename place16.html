A Glimpse into History
Netarhat, meaning "the land of bamboo," has a rich history from ancient tribal kingdoms to British colonial rule, becoming a scenic summer retreat with lasting architectural marvels.

Architectural Splendour
Netarhat's architecture blends colonial and tribal influences, featuring British-era buildings, traditional huts, and the landmark Netarhat Residential School, alongside viewpoints and guesthouses offering serene views for meditation.

Cultural and Spiritual Significance
Netarhat is a spiritual haven, ideal for meditation, with festivals like Sarhul and Karma celebrating nature, community, and divine blessings through music, dance, rituals, and communal feasts.
